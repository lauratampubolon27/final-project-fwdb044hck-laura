About Me and This Website

About Me

Laura is a unique person with passion in books, journaling, and singing. She is usually called Ra or Lala. She has fallen in love with journaling since Elementary School by telling her personal stories in a secret diary, or submitting her poems to a blog. She likes coffee and listening music because it will calm her heart. When she want to release her mind, she will read a book while listening to music and accompanied by a cup of coffee.

About This Website

A website that created because of Laura's love of books and photograph that fills her life. She wants to share things like books and photographs that make her happy to the whole world. She hope everyone also feel her happiness. Please enjoy the website!
